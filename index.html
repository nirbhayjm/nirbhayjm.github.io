Biography

I am a Post-Doctoral Fellow at the Center for Data Science in the Nell Hodgson Woodruff School of Nursing at Emory University, working with Prof. Xiao Hu. Prior to this, I have received my PhD in Computer Science from the School of Interactive Computing within the College of Computing at Georgia Tech, where I was advised by Prof. Dhruv Batra. I have received my bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from IIT Kanpur, where I worked with Prof. Piyush Rai.

My long term goal is to develop machine learning and reinforcement learning algorithms for biomedical data analysis and sequential decision making in medical treatment.

I also enjoy powerlifting, hiking, and speed solving. My official World Cube Association record for fastest single solve of the 3 x 3 Rubik’s Cube is 15.33 seconds.
